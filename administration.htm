Services  »  Shareholder Administration

Dot every “i” and cross every “t”

The offering is only the beginning. For your REIT to remain viable, you have to remain vigilant in administering it and conforming to the many rules and regulations. Entrust REIT Funding to do it for you and secure your status.

We can reduce the chance of compliance issues that can put your REIT at risk by managing and maintaining the accommodation shareholder records necessary to protect your investment.

Our rigorous processes ensure that all IRS requirements related to the 100-shareholder rule are met from initial filing to annual reporting. Employing our comprehensive, proprietary software systems, we efficiently handle all administrative duties, including the mailing and issuance of dividend checks, over the life of the REIT.

We handle the details

Through REIT Administration, LLC, we handle all the tax, compliance and administrative duties related to the shareholders including:

  • Obtaining W-9s from all shareholders
  • Prepare Share Certificates or Notices of Uncertificated Shares
  • IRS reporting related to preparation and filing of 1099s
    • Reporting of any correction which would include changes in category such as ordinary income, return of capital or capital gain
    • Sending of B-Notices, if necessary
    • Backup withholding and deposits on Form 8109-B
    • Filing of Form 945
    • Any state filings, should they be required
  • Ensuring dividends are paid by notifying the REIT in advance and following up as needed
  • Issuance and mailing of individual dividend checks
  • Tracking ownership changes as transfer agent
  • Documenting compliance with 100 shareholder test
  • Providing shareholders with summary tax information in a form suitable for inclusion in their income tax returns
  • Sending shareholder demand letters as dictated by Treas. Reg. 1.857-8(d)(3) and (e) if required
  • Retaining annual shareholder records required under Treas. Reg. Sec. 1.857-8(a) for the preferred shareholders
  • Responding in a timely manner to shareholder inquiries regarding tax and administrative matters so you don’t have to

We serve multiple types of REITs:

  • UBIT Blockers
  • State Tax Planning REITs
  • Taxable Mortgage Pool REITs
  • REITs for offshore investors including Canadian & Dutch Pension Funds, German Open Ended Funds, Australian Property Trusts and Non Treaty Investors
